About Lovely Beauty

Ethiopia's first professional beauty college — since 1998

Our Story

Lovely Beauty College and Training Center is Ethiopia's first fully established professional beauty training center and now a national beauty college. Built on more than two decades of leadership and innovation, Lovely has transformed the beauty industry by creating structured professional education and career pathways.

From a humble beginning in 1998 G.C with only five students, Lovely Beauty College has grown into a multi-branch institution serving thousands of students across Ethiopia.

Today, we operate 4 campuses: Megenagna (Main), Arada, Merkato, and Gofa — each equipped with modern salon labs and training facilities.
