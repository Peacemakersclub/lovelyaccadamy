About Lovely Beauty

Ethiopia's First Private Beauty College — Excellence Since 1998

Our Story

Lovely Plus Beauty College was founded in 1998 with a singular vision: to elevate beauty education in Ethiopia to international standards. As the first private beauty college in the nation, we pioneered professional training in hairdressing, makeup, skincare, and all aspects of the beauty industry.

Over 28 years, we have grown from a single training center to three campuses across Addis Ababa, producing over 5,000 graduates who now work in top salons, run their own businesses, and have found success internationally.
