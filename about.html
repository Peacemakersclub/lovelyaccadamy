Company Profile

Lovely Beauty College and Training Center is Ethiopia's first fully established professional beauty training institution and now a recognized national beauty college. Founded through vision, leadership, and dedication, Lovely Beauty College has played a historic role in shaping Ethiopia's beauty industry.

From its humble beginning in 1998 G.C with only five students, Lovely has grown into a national institution with multiple branches and thousands of graduates across Ethiopia.

Our Story

Lovely Beauty College was founded by visionary leaders who believed beauty is not just talent—it is a professional career that deserves recognition and standards. The institution was established to create a formal, professional beauty education system in Ethiopia.

🏆 A Historic Milestone

In 2000 G.C, Lovely organized Ethiopia's first national beauty competition at Sheraton Addis, awarding professional stylists and introducing beauty as a respected profession. This historic event was broadcast on television, radio, and magazines, bringing national recognition to the beauty industry.
